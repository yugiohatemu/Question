Troll and Goblin
A bunch of goblins are lining up in the forest for lunch. Each goblin only know its own height and the # of goblin in front of it that its taller than it. Suddenly a troll who comes, the goblins get scared and ran away. After the troll gone, the goblins gather to line up. Can you help them line up in the order before the troll came?

Example:
Goblin with ID 1,2,3,4,5 has height respectively 1,2,3,4,5
Say the line up order is E A C B D
Following input is ID Height #of people higher than ID's Height
Input:
1 1 0
2 2 1
3 3 1
4 4 2
5 5 1

Output:
5 1 3 2 4
